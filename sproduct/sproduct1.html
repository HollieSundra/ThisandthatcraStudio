Beautiful butterfly shaped/floral pattern needle minder. This needle minder pulls double duty as a safe place to park your needle and as something pretty to decorate your work in progress! Great for sewing, embroidery, cross stitch, or anything with needles. Strong neodymium magnets will hold this needle minder in place, just place the needle minder on the working side of your project and the other magnet on the back side, it's that simple!
