Who We Are?

We are a small family run business. This and That Craft Studio was started by Carrie Smith and her mother Diane Smith during the Pandemic to help spread their love of sewing and embroidery.

Carrie is the maker of all embroidered and hand sewn products. She enjoys 'slow stitch' and never really mastered using a sewing machine. Her other interests include gardening, antiquing, and going to craft stores.

Diane creates all of the machine sewn products. She learned how to sew by watching her mother as a little girl. Some of her other interests are gardening, cooking, and traveling.
